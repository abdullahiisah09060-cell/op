Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee to apply for an SBA grant?
No. There is absolutely no fee to submit an application through this portal. The SBA grant program is federally funded and free to access. Be cautious of any third party claiming to charge for application assistance.
How long does the review process take?
Most applications are reviewed within 18 to 72 hours of submission. Complex cases or incomplete documentation may require additional review time. You will be notified of any status changes via email and your dashboard.
What documents do I need to complete my application?
Required documents include a government-issued photo ID, proof of address, Social Security Number or EIN, and bank account details for disbursement. Business applicants may also need recent tax filings and a business registration certificate.
How will I receive my approved funds?
Approved funds are disbursed directly to your verified bank account via ACH transfer or wire. You may also elect to hold funds in your secure SBA vault before initiating a withdrawal to your personal account.
Can I apply for more than one grant program?
Yes, eligible applicants may apply to multiple programs provided they meet each program's specific eligibility requirements. Applications are reviewed independently and do not affect one another.
